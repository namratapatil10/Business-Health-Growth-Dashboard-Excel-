Business Health & Growth Dashboard (Excel)
Objective

Create an executive-level dashboard to monitor revenue, orders, average order value, completion rate, top restaurants, and city-wise performance on a monthly basis, helping management track overall business health and growth.

Key KPIs Used

Total Revenue – Total sales amount for the selected period

Total Orders – Total number of orders placed

Average Order Value (AOV) – Revenue ÷ Total Orders

Completed Order Rate (%) – Percentage of successfully completed orders

Visualizations Used

KPI Cards – Total Revenue, Total Orders, Average Order Value, Completed Order Rate

Line Chart – Monthly trend of Revenue and Orders

Bar Chart – Top 10 Restaurants by Revenue

Bar Chart – Revenue by City

Month Slicer – Interactive monthly filtering

Data Handling & Analysis

Cleaned and structured raw order data

Used Pivot Tables to calculate KPIs

Created calculated fields for AOV and completion rate

Enabled dynamic filtering using slicers

Tools & Techniques

Microsoft Excel

Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts

Slicers & Filters

Excel Formulas

Dashboard Design & Formatting

Business Outcome

Provides a quick snapshot of business performance

Helps identify top-performing restaurants and cities

Tracks monthly revenue and order trends

Supports management-level decision-making
